Basketball Positions Guide

Point Guard (PG)

PG

The primary ball handler and playmaker. Responsible for running the offense, distributing the ball, and setting up scoring opportunities for teammates.

Shooting Guard (SG)

G

A perimeter player focused on scoring, especially from outside. Often the team's best shooter and secondary ball handler.

Small Forward (SF)

G

A versatile player who can score inside and outside. Balances perimeter shooting with driving to the basket and defending multiple positions.

Power Forward (PF)

F

A strong interior player who scores near the basket and grabs rebounds. Often plays with their back to the basket and defends in the paint.

Center (C)

C

The tallest player, positioned near the basket. Primary rebounder and rim protector, responsible for defending the paint and scoring close to the hoop.
